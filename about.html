About Everyday Genius

Everyday Genius helps people convert their professional experience and hobbies into small-scale, sustainable income streams. We focus on measurable outcomes, honest guidance, and tools that reduce risk for learners and founders.

Our Approach

We combine practical templates, step-by-step plans, and tool recommendations to help you launch quickly without expensive overhead. Our advisors are product and small-business practitioners who prioritize transparency.

What we teach

  • Lean launch planning
  • Low-cost marketing strategies
  • Simple bookkeeping & compliance basics
